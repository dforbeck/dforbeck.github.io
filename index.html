About me:

I transitioned into software application development via the intensive, immersive program a Eleven Fifty Academy in Fishers, Indiana after starting a PhD program in Biostatistics and realizing that my real career home was in computing. As a result, I am very comfortable with mathematical applications, having taken mathematical courses up to linear algebra, as well as having exposure to graduate level work in statistics and computational statistics. My prior professional background is in marketing and market research (where I did some data mining); I have an MBA from a top 20 program. I have worked in a marketing/market research capacity in the electrical industry, consumer packaged goods industry, and high tech.


On a personal level...


I stayed home for some time to raise my beautiful daughters before continuing on with my professional pursuits. I am an avid fan of jazz and blues music from all eras; having played the flute in my youth, I have a deep appreciation for the wonderful breadth and variety these genres offer. I became a wine afficionado after living in the beautiful San Francisco Bay Area and exploring both Napa and Sonoma counties. And of course, I would be remiss if I didn't mention that I enjoy watching all sorts of cooking shows. Lastly, volunteering has been a large part of my life; I am currently an active Girl Scout volunteer.
