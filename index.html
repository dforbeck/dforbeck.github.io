About me:

I transitioned into software application development via the intensive, immersive program at Eleven Fifty Academy in Fishers, Indiana. I came to Eleven Fifty after starting a PhD program in Biostatistics and realizing that my real career home was in computing. As such, I am very comfortable with mathematical applications, having taken mathematical courses through linear algebra, as well as having exposure to graduate level work in statistics and statistical computing. My prior professional background is in marketing and market research (where I did some data mining). Lastly, I have an MBA from a top 20 business program and have worked in a marketing/market research capacity in the electrical industry, consumer packaged goods industry, and high tech.


On a personal level...


I stayed home for some time to raise my beautiful children before continuing on with my professional pursuits. I am an avid fan of jazz and blues music from all eras; having played the flute in my youth, I have a deep appreciation for the wonderful breadth and variety these genres offer. I became a wine afficionado after living in the beautiful San Francisco Bay Area and exploring both Napa and Sonoma counties. And of course, I would be remiss if I didn't mention that I enjoy watching all sorts of cooking shows. Lastly, volunteering has been a large part of my life; I am currently an active Girl Scout volunteer.
